# Usability Research Findings — January 2025

## Summary

Recent user testing sessions (n=12) conducted January 18-22 reveal significant friction in the payment flow.

## Key Findings

1. **Payment Timeout Frustration**: 8 out of 12 participants experienced payment processing delays exceeding 5 seconds. Several participants expressed frustration and abandoned the checkout.

2. **No Retry Mechanism**: When payment fails, users are redirected back to the cart without a clear retry option. 6 participants were confused about how to attempt payment again.

3. **Missing Error Feedback**: Payment failures show a generic "Something went wrong" message without actionable guidance. Users reported feeling "stuck" and unsure what to do next.

4. **Platform-Specific Issues**: iOS users reported significantly worse payment experiences than Android users. The payment flow appears to be particularly slow on iOS devices.

5. **Competitive Loss Risk**: 3 participants mentioned they would switch to a competitor if payment issues persisted. One user said they had already started using an alternative app.

## Recommendations

- Investigate payment service performance, particularly on iOS
- Add clear retry mechanism for failed payments
- Improve error messaging with specific guidance
- Consider adding alternative payment methods as fallback
